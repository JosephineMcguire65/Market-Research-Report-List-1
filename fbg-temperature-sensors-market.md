<p><strong>The FBG Temperature Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distributed Sensing</li><li>Fiber Optic Point Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The FBG (Fiber Bragg Grating) temperature sensors market comprises two main types: distributed sensing and fiber optic point sensing. Distributed sensing utilizes a single fiber optic cable to measure temperature along its entire length, providing extensive data over large areas. In contrast, fiber optic point sensing focuses on specific locations by embedding multiple FBG sensors at discrete points. Each type offers unique advantages for temperature monitoring, with distributed sensing ideal for large-scale applications and point sensing suited for targeted measurements.</p></p>

<p><p>The orally disintegrating tablet excipient market includes various components that enhance tablet formulation and performance. Binders provide cohesion to ingredients, ensuring tablet integrity. Glidants improve the flow properties of the powder mixture, facilitating uniformity during manufacturing. Diluents, or fillers, increase the tablet size for adequate dosage without affecting active ingredients. Disintegrants promote rapid breakdown of the tablet upon administration, enhancing drug release. Other excipients may include colorants and preservatives, which improve stability and patient compliance.</p></p>

<p><p>The vacuum cryo pumps market encompasses two main types: Injection Liquid Type and Cryopump of Closed Cycle Gas Type. </p><p>Injection Liquid Type pumps utilize liquid helium or nitrogen to achieve low temperatures, condensing gases and enabling high vacuum levels. In contrast, Closed Cycle Gas Type cryopumps use a closed-loop refrigeration system, eliminating the need for external cryogens. Both types are crucial in various applications, including semiconductor manufacturing and scientific research, where ultra-high vacuum conditions are essential.</p></p>

<p><p>A Generator Circuit Breaker (GCB) is a specialized electrical device designed to protect power generators and associated equipment by interrupting fault currents and ensuring safe disconnection during maintenance or emergencies. <p><p>Generator Circuit Breakers (GCBs) are critical components in power systems, protecting generators from faults. The market offers three main types: Plug-in, Fixed, and Drawer. Plug-in GCBs are modular, allowing easy installation and removal for maintenance. Fixed GCBs are permanently mounted, offering robust and reliable protection with minimal maintenance. Drawer GCBs combine features of both, enabling sliding mechanisms for accessibility while maintaining secure connections. Each type caters to specific operational needs, balancing convenience, durability, and efficiency in power generation systems.</p></p>

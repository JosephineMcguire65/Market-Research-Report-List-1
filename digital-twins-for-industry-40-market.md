<p><p>Digital twins in Industry 4.0 encompass three primary market types: Asset Twin, Process Twin, and System Twin. Asset Twin focuses on individual physical assets, providing real-time monitoring and predictive maintenance capabilities. Process Twin simulates manufacturing processes, enabling optimization and efficiency improvements through data analysis. System Twin integrates multiple assets and processes, creating a comprehensive virtual representation of the entire production system. Together, these digital twins enhance decision-making, operational efficiency, and innovation in industrial environments, driving advancements in smart manufacturing.</p></p>

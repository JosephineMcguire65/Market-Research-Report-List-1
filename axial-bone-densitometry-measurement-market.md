<p><strong>The Axial Bone Densitometry Measurement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (DEXA)</li><li>Quantitative Computed Tomography (QCT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Axial bone densitometry measurement encompasses two primary market types: Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (DEXA) and Quantitative Computed Tomography (QCT). DEXA is the gold standard technique, utilizing low-dose X-rays to assess bone mineral density and diagnose conditions like osteoporosis. In contrast, QCT employs advanced CT imaging to provide three-dimensional assessments of bone density, allowing detailed evaluation of skeletal microarchitecture. Both methods are crucial for patient management and risk assessment related to bone health and fractures.</p></p>

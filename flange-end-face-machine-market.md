The Outer Diameter Installation machine is designed for external flange facing operations, utilizing CNC technology for precision and efficiency in machining large flanges. In contrast, the Internally Mounted Flange Facing Machine is engineered for inner flange surfaces, allowing for effective machining in confined spaces. Both types serve critical roles in industries requiring precise flange preparation and maintenance.</p></p>

<p><p>The metal coating market encompasses two main types: liquid coating and powder coating. Liquid coating involves the application of a liquid paint that dries to form a protective layer, offering versatility in finish and color. Powder coating, on the other hand, utilizes a dry powder that is electrostatically charged and baked to create a durable finish. Both methods enhance corrosion resistance, surface aesthetics, and durability, catering to diverse industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics.</p></p>

<p><p>The oil burner market comprises three main types: light oil burners, heavy oil burners, and dual-use burners. Light oil burners are designed for low-viscosity fuels, offering efficient combustion for residential and commercial heating. Heavy oil burners handle high-viscosity fuels, typically used in industrial applications, providing robust performance but requiring more maintenance. Dual-use burners can operate with both light and heavy oils, offering flexibility for varied applications. Each type caters to specific energy demands and operational requirements within different sectors.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Steam Dried Fishmeal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Defatted Fish Meal</li><li>Semi-skimmed Fish Meal</li><li>Whole Fish Meal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam dried fishmeal market consists of three main types: defatted fish meal, semi-skimmed fish meal, and whole fish meal. Defatted fish meal is processed to remove most of the oil, resulting in higher protein content, making it ideal for livestock feed. Semi-skimmed fish meal retains some fat, offering a balance of protein and energy, suitable for various animal diets. Whole fish meal contains all components, providing a comprehensive nutrient source, commonly used in aquaculture and pet food formulations.</p></p>

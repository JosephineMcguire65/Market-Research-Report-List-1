<p><p>The hemorrhagic neurointerventional device market comprises three key types: Coil Embolization Systems, Neurointervention Flow-Diverting Stents, and other specialized devices. Coil Embolization Systems are used to treat aneurysms by occluding blood vessels with coils, thereby preventing hemorrhage. Flow-diverting stents redirect blood flow away from aneurysms, promoting healing and reducing rupture risk. Other devices include balloon angioplasty systems and access catheters, which play supporting roles in neurointerventional procedures, enhancing treatment options for cerebrovascular conditions.</p></p>

<p><p>The elastic laminates market comprises various types, including ethylene-based elastomers, propylene-based elastomers, and styrene-block copolymers. Ethylene-based elastomers are known for their flexibility and durability, making them suitable for packaging and automotive applications. Propylene-based elastomers offer enhanced heat resistance and are often used in industrial and consumer goods. Styrene-block copolymers, characterized by their versatility and processability, are popular in adhesives, sealants, and coatings, providing excellent elasticity and strength in various products.</p></p>

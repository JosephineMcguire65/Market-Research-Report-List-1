<p><strong>The LCD Driver Probe Card Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>General Pitch</li><li>Fine Pitch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LCD driver probe card market is segmented into two primary types: general pitch and fine pitch. General pitch probe cards are designed for standard applications with larger contact spacing, making them suitable for cost-effective testing of less complex LCDs. In contrast, fine pitch probe cards feature closely spaced contacts, allowing for precise testing of advanced LCD technologies, where high resolution and performance are critical. The choice between these types depends on the specific requirements of the LCD testing process.</p></p>

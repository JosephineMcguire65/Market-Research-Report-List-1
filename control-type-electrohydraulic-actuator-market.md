<p><p>The electrohydraulic actuator market consists of two primary types: linear actuators and rotary actuators. Linear actuators convert hydraulic energy into straight-line motion, enabling precise control in applications such as robotics, manufacturing, and aerospace. In contrast, rotary actuators transform hydraulic energy into rotational motion, ideal for tasks requiring torque, such as valve control and machinery operation. Both actuator types are essential in various industries, offering advantages like efficiency, compactness, and enhanced control for complex systems.</p></p>

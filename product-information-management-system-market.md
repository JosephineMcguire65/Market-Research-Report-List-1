<p><p>Product Information Management (PIM) systems can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based PIM solutions are hosted on remote servers, facilitating easier access, scalability, and lower upfront costs, making them ideal for businesses seeking flexibility and quick deployment. On-premises PIM systems, in contrast, are installed within a company's own IT infrastructure, providing greater control over data security and customization but often requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Both options cater to varying organizational needs.</p></p>

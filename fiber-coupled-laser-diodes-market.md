<p><strong>The Fiber Coupled Laser Diodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber coupled laser diodes are categorized into single mode and multimode types based on their coupling with optical fibers. Single mode laser diodes operate with a narrow beam, allowing for long-distance signal transmission with minimal loss and dispersion, making them ideal for telecommunications. In contrast, multimode laser diodes support multiple light paths within a larger core fiber, facilitating higher power and data rates over shorter distances, commonly used in data centers and local area networks. Each type serves specific applications in the optical communication spectrum.</p></p>

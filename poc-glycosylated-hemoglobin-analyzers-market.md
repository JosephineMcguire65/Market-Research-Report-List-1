<p><p>The POC glycosylated hemoglobin analyzers market encompasses various types, including immunoassay and boronate affinity methods. Immunoassays utilize antibody-antigen interactions to detect hemoglobin variants, providing reliable results, while boronate affinity techniques leverage the specific binding of boronic acid to glycosylated hemoglobin to achieve precise measurements. Other methods may involve alternative biochemical techniques or innovative technologies. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of accuracy, speed, and ease of use, catering to diverse clinical settings and patient needs.</p></p>

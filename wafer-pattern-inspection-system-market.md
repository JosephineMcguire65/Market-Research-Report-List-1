<p><strong>The Wafer Pattern Inspection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electron Beam Inspection System</li><li>Bright-Field Inspection System</li><li>Dark-Field Inspection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wafer Pattern Inspection System market includes various technologies utilized for detecting defects on semiconductor wafers. Electron Beam Inspection Systems use focused beams of electrons to achieve high-resolution imaging, ideal for detecting minute defects. Bright-Field Inspection Systems rely on transmitted light to identify surface irregularities, useful for evaluating patterns in photomasks and wafers. In contrast, Dark-Field Inspection Systems utilize scattered light to highlight defects against a dark background, enhancing visibility of subtle flaws. Each type caters to specific inspection requirements in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>

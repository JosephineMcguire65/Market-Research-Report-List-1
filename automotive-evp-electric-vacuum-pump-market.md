<p><p>The Automotive Electric Vacuum Pump (EVP) market includes various types such as Diaphragm, Leaf, and Swing Piston pumps. Diaphragm Type EVPs use a flexible membrane to create vacuum pressure, offering quiet operation and durability. Leaf Type EVPs utilize rotating blades to generate vacuum, providing compact design and efficiency. Swing Piston Type EVPs employ a piston mechanism for high vacuum levels, ensuring robust performance in demanding applications. Each type caters to specific automotive needs, balancing efficiency, noise, and durability for brake boosters and other vacuum-dependent systems.</p></p>

<p><p>The Digital Audio Transmission System market consists of two primary types: wireless-based and wired-based systems. Wireless-based systems use technologies like Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, or radio frequency to transmit audio signals without physical connections, offering flexibility and mobility. In contrast, wired-based systems rely on physical cables, such as HDMI or optical fiber, to deliver high-quality audio with minimal latency and interference. Both types cater to different user needs, balancing convenience and sound fidelity for various applications, from home entertainment to professional audio setups.</p></p>

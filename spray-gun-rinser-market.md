<p><p>The spray gun rinser market is divided into two main types: air spray gun rinsers and water spray gun rinsers. Air spray gun rinsers utilize compressed air to efficiently clean spray guns by blowing away paint residue and debris, promoting quick drying. On the other hand, water spray gun rinsers utilize a water-based cleaning process, making them ideal for water-soluble paints. Both types enhance productivity and maintain equipment hygiene, catering to industries such as automotive painting, industrial coating, and furniture manufacturing.</p></p>

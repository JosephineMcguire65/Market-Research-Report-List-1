IVUS consoles are specialized devices that generate and display ultrasound images during vascular procedures. IVUS catheters are slender tubes equipped with ultrasound technology, inserted into blood vessels to obtain detailed imaging of arterial walls and plaque. IVUS accessories include various supportive tools and components that enhance the functionality and efficiency of IVUS systems, facilitating improved diagnostics and therapeutic interventions in cardiovascular care.</p></p>

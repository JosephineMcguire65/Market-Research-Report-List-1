<p><p>The intrathecal drug delivery market is primarily divided into two types: constant rate pumps and programmable pumps. Constant rate pumps deliver a steady flow of medication directly into the spinal canal, ensuring consistent drug levels for pain management or other treatments. In contrast, programmable pumps offer flexibility, allowing healthcare providers to adjust the dosage or infusion rate based on individual patient needs. Both types enhance targeted therapy while minimizing systemic side effects, ultimately improving patient outcomes in managing chronic conditions.</p></p>

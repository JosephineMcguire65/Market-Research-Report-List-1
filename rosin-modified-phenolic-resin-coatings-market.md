<p><p>The Rosin Modified Phenolic Resin Coatings market encompasses two primary types: dissolving and cooking types. Dissolving type coatings are prepared by dissolving rosin in phenolic resins, yielding products with quick-drying and excellent adhesion properties, ideal for various applications. On the other hand, cooking type coatings involve the combination of rosin and phenolic resin through heat treatment, resulting in enhanced durability and thermal resistance. Both types are widely used in protective and decorative coatings across industries, including automotive and construction.</p></p>

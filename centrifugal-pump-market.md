<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Centrifugal Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Flow Pumps</li><li>Mixed or Radial Flow Pumps</li><li>Peripheral Pumps</li><li>Jet Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The centrifugal pump market is categorized into several types based on design and application. Axial flow pumps move fluid along the pump axis, ideal for high flow rates with low pressure. Mixed or radial flow pumps combine axial and radial flow characteristics, offering versatility for various applications. Peripheral pumps, also known as turbine pumps, handle small flow rates at high pressures, suitable for household or agricultural use. Jet pumps utilize a jet of fluid to create suction, commonly employed in shallow well applications.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Passive Loop Filters</li><li>Active Loop Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Loop filters are essential components in phase-locked loops (PLLs) used in various electronic applications, including communication systems. Passive loop filters use resistors, capacitors, and inductors to manage noise and maintain stability without the need for external power, making them simple and reliable. Active loop filters, on the other hand, incorporate operational amplifiers, allowing for improved performance, adjustable bandwidth, and enhanced control over signal processing. Both types are crucial in maintaining signal integrity and synchronization in electronics.</p></p>

Double Acting pins allow locking in both directions, enhancing versatility in applications. Detent Pins incorporate a mechanism that holds the pin in place until released, ensuring secure locking during operation. These types cater to diverse aircraft assembly and maintenance needs, ensuring reliability and efficiency.</p></p>

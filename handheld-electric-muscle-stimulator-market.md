<p><p>The handheld electric muscle stimulator market includes several types of devices designed to provide various therapeutic effects. Neuromuscular Electrical Stimulation (NMES) targets muscle contractions for rehabilitation. Functional Electrical Stimulation (FES) aids in functional movement for individuals with disabilities. Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS) alleviates pain by stimulating nerves. Burst Mode Alternating Current (BMAC) offers muscle relaxation and pain relief through alternating currents. Microcurrent Electrical Neuro Muscular Stimulators (MENS) utilize low-level currents for enhanced healing. Others may include devices with unique functionalities.</p></p>

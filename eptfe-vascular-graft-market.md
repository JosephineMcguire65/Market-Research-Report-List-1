<p><p>The ePTFE vascular graft market is categorized into small caliber and large caliber types. Small caliber grafts, typically ranging from 4 mm to 6 mm in diameter, are used for applications like peripheral vascular surgery and coronary artery bypass, offering flexibility and enhanced healing. Large caliber grafts, usually over 6 mm in diameter, are employed in arterial repairs and large vessel reconstruction. These grafts provide structural support and improve blood flow, catering to diverse surgical needs in vascular interventions.</p></p>

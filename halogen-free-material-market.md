<p><p>The halogen-free material market is characterized by three key types. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) halogen-free materials are known for their flexibility, durability, and chemical resistance, making them ideal for various applications. PPO (Polyphenylene Oxide) halogen-free materials offer excellent thermal stability and electrical properties, suitable for electrical components and automotive uses. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomers, excluding TPU) halogen-free materials provide rubber-like properties and ease of processing, making them popular for consumer goods and industrial applications, ensuring safety and environmental compliance.</p></p>

<p><strong>The VR Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Fresnel Lens</li><li>Fresnel Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VR lens market encompasses two primary types: non-Fresnel and Fresnel lenses. Non-Fresnel lenses are traditional, thicker optical lenses that provide high image quality but can be bulkier and heavier. In contrast, Fresnel lenses utilize thin, lightweight designs with a series of concentric grooves, allowing for compact form factors ideal for VR headsets. While non-Fresnel lenses deliver superior clarity, Fresnel lenses offer advantages in portability and comfort, catering to diverse consumer preferences in virtual reality experiences.</p></p>

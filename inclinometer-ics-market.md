<p><p>Inclinometer ICs, used for measuring tilt and orientation, come in two primary market types: Surface Mount and Through Hole. Surface Mount ICs are designed for direct mounting on the surface of circuit boards, facilitating compact designs and automated production processes. In contrast, Through Hole ICs have pins that pass through holes in the circuit board, providing robust connections and ease of manual assembly. Each type caters to specific application needs, balancing space constraints and durability.</p></p>

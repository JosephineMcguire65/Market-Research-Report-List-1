<p><strong>The Supplementary Cementitious Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Supplementary Cementitious Material (SCM) market is primarily categorized into two types: solid and powder. Solid SCMs typically include materials like fly ash and slag granulate that are used in bulk form, enhancing concrete properties while minimizing environmental impact. Powder SCMs, on the other hand, consist of finely ground materials such as silica fume and metakaolin, which improve strength and durability. Both types are essential for sustainable construction practices, reducing the need for traditional cement and lowering carbon emissions.</p></p>

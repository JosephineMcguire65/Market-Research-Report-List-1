<p><ul><li>Whole Body Cryo</li><li>Local Cryo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cryoanalgesia equipment market is divided into two main types: whole body cryotherapy and local cryotherapy. Whole body cryotherapy involves exposing the entire body to extremely low temperatures, promoting overall pain relief, recovery, and wellness. In contrast, local cryotherapy targets specific areas of the body to alleviate localized pain and inflammation, using devices that apply cold directly to affected tissues. Both methods leverage cold temperature technology to manage pain and enhance therapeutic outcomes in various clinical settings.</p></p>

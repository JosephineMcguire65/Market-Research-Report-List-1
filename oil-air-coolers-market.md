<p><strong>The Oil-air Coolers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-type</li><li>Dry-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil-air coolers market is segmented into two primary types: oil-type and dry-type coolers. Oil-type coolers utilize oil as a heat transfer medium, efficiently reducing the temperature of engine or industrial fluids. They are commonly employed in hydraulic systems and heavy machinery. Dry-type coolers, on the other hand, use air for cooling without any liquid medium, making them suitable for environments where liquid cooling is impractical. Both types are crucial for maintaining optimal operating temperatures in various industrial applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The automotive wiper blades market is categorized into three main types: boneless, bone, and hybrid wiper blades. Boneless wiper blades feature a sleek design without a traditional frame, offering improved aerodynamics and a clearer view. Bone wiper blades come with a metal or plastic frame, providing durability and effective performance. Hybrid wiper blades combine features from both boneless and bone designs, ensuring enhanced flexibility and functionality, making them versatile for various weather conditions. Each type caters to specific consumer preferences and vehicle requirements.</p></p>

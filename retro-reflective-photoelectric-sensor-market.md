<p><p>The retro-reflective photoelectric sensor market is segmented into two main types: separate and compact sensors. Separate sensors consist of a transmitter and a receiver that can be placed at varying distances, allowing for greater flexibility in setup and application. In contrast, compact sensors integrate both components into a single unit, making them ideal for space-constrained environments. Both types serve essential roles in automation and safety applications, utilizing light reflection principles to detect objects effectively.</p></p>

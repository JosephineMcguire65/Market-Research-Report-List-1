<p><p>The tire flaps market encompasses two primary types: radial tire flaps and bias tire flaps. Radial tire flaps are designed to support radial tires, providing enhanced performance and stability by distributing pressure evenly across the tire's surface. In contrast, bias tire flaps are tailored for bias-ply tires, offering flexibility and durability, often preferred in specific applications like heavy-duty vehicles. Both types are essential for maintaining tire integrity, improving safety, and enhancing overall vehicle performance across various industries.</p></p>

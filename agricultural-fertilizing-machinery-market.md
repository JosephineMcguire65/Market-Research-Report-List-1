<p><p>The agricultural fertilizing machinery market primarily includes two key types: spreaders and sprayers. Spreaders distribute dry fertilizers uniformly across fields, ensuring even nutrient application for crops. They come in various forms, such as gravity-feed and pneumatic types. Sprayers, on the other hand, apply liquid fertilizers or pesticides, allowing for targeted treatment and better foliage coverage. Both machines enhance agricultural efficiency, improve crop yield, and optimize resource use, playing vital roles in modern farming practices.</p></p>

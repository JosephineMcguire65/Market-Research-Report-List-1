<p><strong>The NFC Antenna Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-way Transmission</li><li>Arbitrary Direction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NFC antenna module market is categorized into two main types: one-way transmission and arbitrary direction. One-way transmission allows data to be sent from an NFC-enabled device to a reader, suitable for applications like access control and payment systems. In contrast, the arbitrary direction market enables bi-directional communication between devices without a specified orientation, enhancing usability in various applications, such as smart home devices and wearables. Both types cater to distinct use cases and user requirements in the evolving NFC landscape.</p></p>

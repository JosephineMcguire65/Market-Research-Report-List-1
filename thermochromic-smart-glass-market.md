<p><ul><li>Low E-Glasses</li><li>Active Glasses</li><li>Passive Glasses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermochromic smart glass market can be categorized into three main types: Low E-Glasses, Active Glasses, and Passive Glasses. Low E-Glasses feature a low emissivity coating that reflects heat while allowing natural light, enhancing energy efficiency. Active Glasses can change transparency dynamically in response to temperature changes or light exposure, offering adaptability in various environments. Passive Glasses utilize intrinsic thermochromic materials that respond to temperature shifts without external stimuli, providing a more stable solution for managing solar heat gain.</p></p>

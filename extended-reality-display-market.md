<p><ul><li>Liquid Crystal Displays (LCD)</li><li>Organic Light-Emitting Diode (OLED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Extended Reality Display Market encompasses various technologies, primarily Liquid Crystal Displays (LCD) and Organic Light-Emitting Diode (OLED) displays. LCDs utilize liquid crystals and a backlight to produce images, offering high brightness and cost-effectiveness, making them ideal for large displays. Conversely, OLED displays use organic compounds to emit light directly, providing superior color accuracy, deeper blacks, and wider viewing angles, making them preferable for immersive experiences. Both technologies play crucial roles in enhancing user engagement in extended reality applications.</p></p>

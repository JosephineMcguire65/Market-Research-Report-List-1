<p><p>Compact filters are categorized based on their efficiency in capturing particulate matter, defined by ISO standards. The ISO ePM1, ISO ePM2.5, and ISO ePM10 filters denote their ability to trap particles of different sizes: ePM1 targets particles smaller than 1 micron, ePM2.5 focuses on those smaller than 2.5 microns, and ePM10 captures particles smaller than 10 microns. These filters are essential in various applications, ensuring air quality and compliance with regulations, ultimately improving health and environmental standards.</p></p>

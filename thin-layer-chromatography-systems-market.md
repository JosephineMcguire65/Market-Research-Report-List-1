<p><p>The Thin Layer Chromatography Systems market is categorized into three types: Classical TLC, High-Performance TLC (HPTLC), and Preparative TLC (PLC). Classical TLC involves basic stationary and mobile phases for qualitative analysis. HPTLC enhances resolution and sensitivity, enabling simultaneous multi-sample analysis with advanced detection techniques. Preparative TLC focuses on large-scale separations for purification, allowing for the isolation of compounds for further use. Each type serves distinct applications in research, pharmaceuticals, and quality control, catering to diverse analytical needs.</p></p>

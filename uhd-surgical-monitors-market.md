<p><p>The UHD surgical monitors market features primarily two types of displays: LED UHD and LCD UHD. LED UHD displays utilize light-emitting diodes for backlighting, offering enhanced brightness, contrast, and color accuracy, making them ideal for surgical settings where detail is crucial. On the other hand, LCD UHD displays use liquid crystal technology, providing reliable image quality and consistent performance. Both types cater to the growing demand for high-resolution visuals in operating rooms, enhancing surgical precision and decision-making.</p></p>

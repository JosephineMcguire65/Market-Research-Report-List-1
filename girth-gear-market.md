<p><ul><li>SpurGirthGear</li><li>HelicalGirthGear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The girth gear market comprises primarily two types: spur girth gears and helical girth gears. Spur girth gears feature straight teeth mounted parallel to the axis, allowing for efficient power transmission, typically used in applications like kilns and mills. In contrast, helical girth gears have angled teeth, providing smoother engagement and higher load capacity, which reduces noise and vibration. Both types are essential in various industries, including cement, mining, and power generation, contributing to the efficiency of large machinery.</p></p>

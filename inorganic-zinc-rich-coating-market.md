<p><strong>The Inorganic Zinc Rich Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Coatings</li><li>Water-based Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inorganic zinc rich coating market is divided into solvent-based and water-based coatings. Solvent-based coatings utilize organic solvents for application, offering robust protection against corrosion and suitable for harsh environments. They typically provide faster drying times and excellent adhesion but may pose environmental concerns due to volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, water-based coatings use water as a solvent, making them more environmentally friendly with lower VOC emissions. While they may have slower drying times, they provide good corrosion resistance and ease of application.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Snow Melting Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HydronicSnowmeltSystems</li><li>ElectricSnowmeltSystems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The snow melting controllers market can be divided into two primary types: hydronic snow melt systems and electric snow melt systems. Hydronic systems utilize heated water circulating through pipes beneath the surface to efficiently melt snow and ice, making them ideal for larger areas. Electric snow melt systems use radiant heat from electric cables installed under the surface, providing quick and localized melting. Both systems enhance safety and accessibility by preventing snow accumulation in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>

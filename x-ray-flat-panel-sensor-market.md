<p><strong>The X-Ray Flat Panel Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect Type</li><li>Direct Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-Ray Flat Panel Sensor market is categorized into two main types: indirect and direct. Indirect type sensors utilize a two-step process, converting X-rays into light, which is then captured by a photosensitive layer. This type often offers better image quality and is more sensitive, making it suitable for various applications. In contrast, direct type sensors convert X-rays directly into electrical signals, providing immediate image capture and reducing processing time, thus enhancing workflow efficiency in medical settings.</p></p>

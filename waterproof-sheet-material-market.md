<p><p>The waterproof sheet material market features various types, including polyester filler (PY), glass fiber filler (G), and glass fiber reinforced polyester filler (PYG). Polyester filler (PY) offers good flexibility and durability for standard waterproof applications. Glass fiber filler (G) enhances strength and resistance to weather conditions, making it suitable for more demanding environments. Glass fiber reinforced polyester filler (PYG) combines the benefits of both materials, providing superior structural integrity and increased resistance to UV and moisture, ideal for heavy-duty projects.</p></p>

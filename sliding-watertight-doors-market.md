<p><p>The sliding watertight doors market encompasses three main types: hydraulic, electric, and pneumatic watertight doors. Hydraulic watertight doors utilize fluid pressure for operation, ensuring robust sealing under high water pressure. Electric watertight doors rely on electric motors for opening and closing, offering precise control and automation. Pneumatic watertight doors operate using compressed air, providing quick action and effective sealing. Each type is designed for specific applications in marine, industrial, and infrastructure settings where water ingress prevention is critical.</p></p>

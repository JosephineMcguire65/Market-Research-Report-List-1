<p><strong>The Drinking Fountains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>BWC</li><li>POU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drinking fountains market is primarily segmented into two types: Bottle-Filling Stations (BWC) and Point-of-Use (POU) systems. BWC units are designed for filling reusable water bottles, promoting sustainability and convenience in public spaces. These fountains often incorporate filtration and cooling features. POU systems, on the other hand, provide direct access to filtered tap water, commonly used in office settings and homes. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers seeking clean drinking water while reducing plastic waste.</p></p>

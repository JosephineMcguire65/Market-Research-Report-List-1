<p><p>The car wheel rims market is divided into **tubeless wheel rims** and **tube wheel rims**. Tubeless rims are designed to hold air directly, offering better safety, fuel efficiency, and reduced maintenance due to fewer punctures. Tube rims require an inner tube to hold air, making them more prone to sudden deflation but often more affordable and suitable for older or specific vehicle models. Both types cater to different vehicle needs, balancing performance, cost, and compatibility.</p></p>

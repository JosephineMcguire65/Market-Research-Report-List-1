<p><strong>The OLED Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode(AMOLED Display)</li><li>Passive Matrix OLED(PMOLED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED display market comprises two primary types: Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode (AMOLED) and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED). AMOLED displays utilize a thin-film transistor system to control individual pixels, enabling high-resolution images and faster refresh rates, making them ideal for smartphones and high-end televisions. In contrast, PMOLED technology illuminates rows and columns for pixel control, resulting in simpler designs and lower costs, suitable for smaller screens like wearable devices and basic displays. Each serves distinct market needs based on performance and application.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Custom Car Wrap Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Film</li><li>Calendered Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The custom car wrap film market is primarily divided into two types: cast film and calendered film. Cast film is produced by pouring molten PVC onto a surface, resulting in a thinner and more flexible material, ideal for intricate designs and contours. In contrast, calendered film is manufactured by passing PVC through rollers, creating a thicker and more durable film that is suited for larger, flatter surfaces. Both types offer distinct advantages for vehicle customization, catering to different aesthetic and performance needs.</p></p>

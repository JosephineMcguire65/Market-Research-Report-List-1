<p><p>The proton therapy instrument market encompasses various technologies for delivering targeted radiation treatment. Synchrotrons are large, complex machines that accelerate protons using magnetic fields, providing high-energy beams. Cyclotrons are compact devices that generate protons in a spiral motion, suitable for smaller clinical setups. Synchrocyclotrons adjust the frequency of the electric field as protons gain speed, optimizing treatment efficiency. Linear accelerators use electromagnetic fields to produce high-energy protons, offering versatile and adaptable treatment options for cancer patients.</p></p>

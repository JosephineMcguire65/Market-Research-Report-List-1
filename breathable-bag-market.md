<p><ul><li>Micro Perforation</li><li>Macro Perforation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The breathable bag market is divided into two main types based on perforation size: micro perforation and macro perforation. Micro perforated bags feature tiny holes that allow air to circulate while minimizing moisture loss, making them ideal for fresh produce and perishable goods. In contrast, macro perforated bags have larger holes, promoting greater air exchange, suitable for items requiring higher airflow, such as baked goods. Both types enhance product shelf life and maintain quality through optimized respiration and moisture management.</p></p>

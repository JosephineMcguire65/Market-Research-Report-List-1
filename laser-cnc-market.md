<p><p>The Laser CNC market can be categorized into Medium Power Control and High Power Control segments. Medium Power Control systems typically operate within the range of 100 to 500 watts, making them suitable for applications like engraving and cutting thin materials. In contrast, High Power Control systems, generally exceeding 500 watts, are designed for more demanding tasks such as heavy-duty metal cutting and welding. Both segments cater to diverse industries, providing tailored solutions based on material type and thickness requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>The Follicle Stimulating Hormone (FSH) market is primarily divided into two types: Recombinant FSH and Urinary FSH. Recombinant FSH is produced through recombinant DNA technology, ensuring high purity and consistent potency, making it widely preferred for fertility treatments and assisted reproductive technologies. In contrast, Urinary FSH is derived from the urine of postmenopausal women and contains FSH along with several other hormones, presenting variability in dosage and purity. Both types are essential for managing reproductive health and stimulating ovarian function.</p></p>

<p><p>The automotive cold forgings market consists of three key types: Custom Forging, Captive Forging, and Catalog Forging. Custom Forging is tailored to specific customer requirements, offering designs unique to each client. Captive Forging occurs when manufacturers produce forgings for their own use, minimizing reliance on third-party suppliers. Catalog Forging provides standardized parts listed in a catalog, allowing for quick and cost-effective procurement for various automotive applications. Each type serves distinct needs within the automotive industry, contributing to efficiency and quality.</p></p>

<p><p>The car brake pads market comprises various types tailored for specific performance needs. Non-asbestos organic (NAO) brake pads prioritize quiet operation and low dust, using organic materials. Low metallic NAO pads enhance braking performance with added metal for better heat dissipation while maintaining low noise levels. Semi-metallic brake pads incorporate a mix of metal fibers for improved durability and heat resistance, often producing more noise. Ceramic brake pads, known for their longevity and low dust production, offer smooth and quiet braking.</p></p>

<p><p>The temperature management market is divided into two main types: patient warming systems and patient cooling systems. Patient warming systems are designed to maintain or elevate a patient's body temperature during surgical procedures or in critical care settings, using blankets, heaters, and other devices. Conversely, patient cooling systems aim to reduce body temperature, often employed in instances of hyperthermia, post-cardiac arrest, or to minimize neurological damage. Both systems are crucial for optimizing patient outcomes and enhancing recovery processes in medical environments.</p></p>

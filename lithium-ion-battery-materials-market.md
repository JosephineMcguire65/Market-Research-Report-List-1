<p><p>The lithium-ion battery materials market comprises several key components: </p><p>1. **Cathode Material** includes lithium metal oxides like lithium cobalt oxide or lithium iron phosphate, which store and release lithium ions. </p><p>2. **Anode Materials** primarily consist of graphite and silicon-based materials, crucial for charge storage. </p><p>3. **Lithium-Ion Battery Separator** is a permeable film that prevents physical contact between electrodes while allowing ion flow. </p><p>4. **Electrolyte** serves as a medium for ion transfer, typically involving lithium salts dissolved in organic solvents, enhancing battery performance and safety.</p></p>

<p><p>The PVC foams market is primarily divided into two types: PVC crosslinked foams and PVC non-crosslinked foams. </p><p>PVC crosslinked foams are created through a chemical process that enhances their durability, heat resistance, and mechanical properties, making them suitable for demanding applications like automotive and construction. </p><p>In contrast, PVC non-crosslinked foams are produced without this chemical process, resulting in lighter, more flexible materials often used in packaging, insulation, and leisure products. Each type caters to specific industry needs and applications.</p></p>

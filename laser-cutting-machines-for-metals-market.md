<p><p>Laser cutting machines for metals include several types, each suited for specific applications. CO2 laser cutting machines are versatile and effective for cutting non-metals and thin metals, using a gas laser to produce high-quality cuts. Fiber laser cutting machines offer high efficiency and speed, ideal for thick metal sheets, due to their solid-state laser technology. YAG (Yttrium Aluminum Garnet) cutting machines are known for precision in cutting reflective materials but are generally less popular compared to CO2 and fiber lasers.</p></p>

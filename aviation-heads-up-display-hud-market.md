<p><p>The Aviation Heads-Up Display (HUD) market comprises two primary types: Conventional HUD and Augmented Reality (AR)-Based HUD. Conventional HUD projects critical flight information onto the cockpit windshield, enhancing pilot situational awareness without shifting focus from the outside environment. In contrast, AR-Based HUD integrates digital content with real-world views, offering dynamic overlays of information like navigation cues or obstacle warnings. This advanced technology enhances immersive experiences, improving decision-making and operational safety for pilots by seamlessly blending data with the cockpit environment.</p></p>

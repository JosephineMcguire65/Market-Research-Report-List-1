<p><p>The Laboratory Information System (LIS) market is categorized into three main types: on-premise, web-based, and cloud-based solutions. On-premise LIS involves software installed locally on laboratory servers, offering high control and customization but requiring significant IT resources. Web-based LIS operates via a browser, facilitating easy access and updates without extensive local infrastructure. Cloud-based LIS utilizes third-party servers, providing scalability, reduced costs, and seamless integration while promoting collaboration across multiple locations, ideal for modern laboratory environments.</p></p>

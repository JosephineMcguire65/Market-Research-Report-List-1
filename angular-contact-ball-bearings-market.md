<p><p>Angular contact ball bearings are classified based on their contact angles, which influence their load-carrying capacity and performance. A 15° angle is ideal for moderate axial loads and essential for high-speed applications. A 25° angle offers a balance for axial and radial loads, commonly used in machine tools. The 30° angle enhances axial load capacity, suitable for gearboxes. A 40° angle is designed for heavy axial loads, ideal for high-performance systems. The "Other" category encompasses specialized angles for unique applications.</p></p>

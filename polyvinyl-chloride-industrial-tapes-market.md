<p><p>The Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) industrial tapes market includes single-sided and double-sided tapes. Single-sided PVC tapes feature adhesive on one side, ideal for insulation, bundling, and surface protection. They are durable, weather-resistant, and widely used in electrical and construction industries. Double-sided PVC tapes have adhesive on both sides, enabling bonding between two surfaces. They are commonly used in mounting, splicing, and assembly applications, offering strong adhesion and flexibility. Both types cater to industrial needs, ensuring reliability and efficiency in diverse applications.</p></p>

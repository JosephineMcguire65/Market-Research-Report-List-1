<p><p>The photonic detectors market encompasses various technologies used to detect and measure light. Photoconductive detectors operate by changing their electrical conductivity in response to light exposure, making them suitable for applications like infrared sensing. Photovoltaic detectors, on the other hand, generate an electric current directly when exposed to light, typically utilized in solar cells and optical sensing. Both types play crucial roles in advancing communication systems, environmental monitoring, and consumer electronics, contributing significantly to the growth of the photonic industry.</p></p>

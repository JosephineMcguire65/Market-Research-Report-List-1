<p><p>Travelator markets can be classified into three types: parallel, multi-parallel, and others. The parallel travelator market features systems arranged side-by-side, allowing multiple users to move concurrently, enhancing efficiency in crowded areas. The multi-parallel market contains several travelators operating independently, often designed to accommodate varying traffic flows and ensuring smoother transitions between levels or sections. Other markets may include specialized designs, such as inclined or curved travelators, catering to unique environments, ensuring versatility in urban transit solutions.</p></p>

<p><p>The magnesium raw materials market comprises various types, primarily focusing on phanerocrystalline and cryptocrystalline magnesite. Phanerocrystalline magnesite features large, visible crystals that enhance its physical properties, making it suitable for high-temperature applications and refractory materials. In contrast, cryptocrystalline magnesite consists of fine-grained crystals, offering greater purity and a more homogeneous structure, ideal for chemical processes and specialized applications. Together, these forms cater to diverse industrial needs, driving the demand for magnesium compounds across various sectors.</p></p>

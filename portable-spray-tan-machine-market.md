<p><strong>The Portable Spray Tan Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Volume Low Pressure (LVLP)</li><li>High Volume Low Pressure (HVLP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable spray tan machine market features two main types: Low Volume Low Pressure (LVLP) and High Volume Low Pressure (HVLP). LVLP machines use less air pressure and produce finer mist, resulting in a more precise application with minimal overspray, ideal for detailed work. Conversely, HVLP machines deliver a higher volume of spray with controlled pressure, covering larger areas quickly, making them suitable for high-demand environments. Each type caters to different user preferences and operational needs in the tanning industry.</p></p>

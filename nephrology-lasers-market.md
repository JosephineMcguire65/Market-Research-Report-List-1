<p><p>The nephrology lasers market features various types, primarily including pulse-dye lasers and fiber lasers. Pulse-dye lasers emit short, high-energy pulses of light, effectively targeting vascular lesions in the kidneys while minimizing damage to surrounding tissues. Fiber lasers utilize optical fibers to deliver concentrated laser energy, providing precision in kidney stone treatment and other urological procedures. Both types offer significant advantages in minimally invasive treatments, enhancing patient outcomes and expanding the applications of laser technology in nephrology.</p></p>

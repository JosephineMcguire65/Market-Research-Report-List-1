<p><p>Manual revolving doors are categorized primarily into two types: three-wing and four-wing designs. The three-wing type features three rotating panels, allowing for efficient pedestrian flow while conserving energy and maintaining a controlled environment. This design is ideal for locations with moderate traffic. In contrast, the four-wing type comprises four panels, offering enhanced security and airlock capabilities, making it suitable for high-traffic areas or environments requiring better climate control. Both designs enhance accessibility and aesthetics in commercial spaces.</p></p>

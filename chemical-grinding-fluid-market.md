<p><p>The chemical grinding fluid market can be categorized into two main types: Mechanical Action Polishing Fluid and Chemical Action Polishing Fluid. Mechanical Action Polishing Fluids primarily rely on abrasive particles to remove material through physical interaction, providing a uniform finish. In contrast, Chemical Action Polishing Fluids utilize chemical reactions to dissolve or smooth surfaces, enhancing the polishing effect and reducing wear on tools. Both types are essential in various industrial applications, contributing to improved surface quality and machining efficiency.</p></p>

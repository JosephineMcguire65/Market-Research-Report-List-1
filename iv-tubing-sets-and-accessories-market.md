<p><p>The IV tubing sets and accessories market comprises three key types based on catheter insertion techniques: Peripheral Intravenous Catheter (PIVC) insertion, Central Venous Catheter (CVC) placement, and Peripherally Inserted Central Catheter (PICC) line insertion. PIVC is typically used for short-term access, CVCs are designed for long-term use in larger veins, while PICC lines provide a minimally invasive method for extended vascular access, often for patients requiring prolonged therapy. Each type serves distinct clinical needs and patient care scenarios.</p></p>

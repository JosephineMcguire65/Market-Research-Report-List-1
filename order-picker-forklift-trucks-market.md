<p><p>Order picker forklift trucks are categorized based on their lifting height capabilities. Low-level order pickers operate up to 3 meters, ideal for retrieving items from shelving and performing tasks in tight spaces or lower racking. Medium-level pickers, spanning 3 to 8 meters, are suited for moderate heights, allowing operators to pick from both low and mid-level shelving efficiently. High-level order pickers exceed 8 meters, designed for reaching the highest storage levels in warehouses, maximizing vertical space utilization and enhancing order fulfillment speed.</p></p>

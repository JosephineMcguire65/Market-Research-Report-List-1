<p><ul><li>Outside Antifoggant</li><li>Within Antifoggant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antifogging agent market is categorized into two main types: outside antifogging agents and within antifogging agents. Outside antifogging agents are applied to the surface of materials such as lenses or glass to create a barrier that prevents fogging. In contrast, within antifogging agents are integrated during the manufacturing process of materials, allowing them to maintain clarity internally and resist fog formation from within. Both types are crucial for enhancing visibility in various applications, including automotive, eyewear, and protective gear.</p></p>

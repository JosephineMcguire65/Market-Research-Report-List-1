<p><p>The cardiac biomarkers market comprises several key types used for diagnosing and monitoring heart conditions. Troponin is a primary marker for myocardial infarction, indicating heart muscle damage. CK-MB also assesses heart injury but is less specific. Natriuretic peptides, such as BNP and NT-proBNP, help evaluate heart failure severity. Myoglobin serves as an early indicator of heart damage. Additionally, other biomarkers may include cardiac-specific enzymes and proteins, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and improving patient management in cardiovascular care.</p></p>

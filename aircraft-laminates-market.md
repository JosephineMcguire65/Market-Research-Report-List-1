<p><p>The Aircraft Laminates market comprises Film Laminates and Reinforced Laminates, each serving distinct roles in aerospace applications. Film Laminates are lightweight, flexible layers used for surfaces requiring corrosion resistance and aesthetic finishes. In contrast, Reinforced Laminates incorporate layers of stronger materials, such as fiberglass or carbon fiber, enhancing structural integrity and load-bearing capacities.

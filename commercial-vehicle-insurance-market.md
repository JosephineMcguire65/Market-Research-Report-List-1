<p><ul><li>Compulsory Insurance</li><li>Optional Insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial vehicle insurance comprises two main market types: compulsory insurance and optional insurance. Compulsory insurance is mandated by law, requiring businesses to obtain minimum coverage to protect against third-party liabilities arising from accidents. This ensures basic financial protection for victims. Optional insurance, on the other hand, allows businesses to choose additional coverage types, like comprehensive or collision insurance, tailored to their specific needs. This flexibility helps companies manage risks effectively while safeguarding their assets and operations from potential losses.</p></p>

<p><p>The Semiconductor Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) valves market is primarily divided into two types: pneumatic actuated and electric actuated valves. Pneumatic actuated valves utilize compressed air to control flow and are favored for their quick response times and reliability in high-pressure environments. In contrast, electric actuated valves use electrical signals for operation, offering precise control and integration with digital systems. Both types are essential in ALD processes, impacting film uniformity and overall deposition efficiency in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>

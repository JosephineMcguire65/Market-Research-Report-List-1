<p><ul><li>Shoulder Cleaner</li><li>Plain Track Cleaner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ballast cleaner market is divided into two main types: shoulder cleaners and plain track cleaners. Shoulder cleaners are designed to maintain the ballast on the sides of the track, effectively removing debris and ensuring proper drainage. Plain track cleaners focus on the ballast directly beneath the rail, removing contaminants to improve track stability and performance. Both types play a crucial role in railway maintenance, enhancing safety, extending the lifespan of the track infrastructure, and optimizing operational efficiency.</p></p>

<p><p>The automotive air cleaner market is primarily categorized into two types: dry type and wet type air cleaners. Dry type air cleaners utilize paper or foam filters to trap dust and contaminants, requiring periodic replacement. They are known for their efficiency and low maintenance. Wet type air cleaners, on the other hand, use an oil-soaked filter media to capture particles, often providing reusable options after cleaning. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing engine performance and longevity by ensuring clean air intake.</p></p>

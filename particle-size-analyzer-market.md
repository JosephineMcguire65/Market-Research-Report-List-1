<p><p>The particle size analyzer market encompasses several key techniques. Laser diffraction measures particle size distribution through light scattering patterns. Dynamic light scattering analyzes the Brownian motion of particles, providing information on size at the nanoscale. Imaging analysis captures and evaluates particle morphology using advanced imaging techniques. The Coulter principle counts and sizes particles as they pass through a conductive liquid. Nanoparticle tracking analysis uses a laser to visualize and size nanoparticles in real time. Other methods include various specialized techniques addressing specific applications.</p></p>

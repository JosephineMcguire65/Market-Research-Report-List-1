<p><p>The automotive inhibitor switch market comprises two main types: linear type and rotary type. Linear type switches operate through a straight motion mechanism, typically used in automatic transmission applications to communicate gear position and ensure safe operation. Rotary type switches, on the other hand, utilize a circular motion to achieve the same purpose, offering precision and reliability in gear selection. Both types are integral for enhancing vehicle safety, performance, and functionality by preventing unintended gear shifts.</p></p>

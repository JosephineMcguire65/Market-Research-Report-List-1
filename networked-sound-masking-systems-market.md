<p><p>Networked sound masking systems are categorized into three main types: In Ceiling Systems, Under Floor Systems, and Others. In Ceiling Systems are installed within the ceiling grid, offering seamless integration and aesthetic appeal while efficiently distributing sound. Under Floor Systems utilize the plenum space beneath raised floors, providing effective sound masking with minimal visual impact. The Others category encompasses alternative configurations, such as wall-mounted systems or standalone units, catering to specific acoustic needs and architectural constraints in various environments.</p></p>

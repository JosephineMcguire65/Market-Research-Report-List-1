<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Mobile Wallet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open and Semi-Open</li><li>Semi-Closed and Closed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile wallets can be categorized into four types based on their market structure. Open wallets allow users to store and spend money across multiple merchants and transfer funds to bank accounts. Semi-open wallets are limited to specific merchants but enable in-app purchases and money transfers. Semi-closed wallets restrict funds to select merchants, often providing incentive programs. Closed wallets operate within a single platform, where funds can only be used for transaction services offered by that platform, enhancing user loyalty.</p></p>

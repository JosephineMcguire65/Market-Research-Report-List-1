<p><p>Geo-marketing encompasses two primary market types: indoor and outdoor. Indoor geo-marketing focuses on engaging consumers within enclosed spaces, utilizing technologies like beacons and Wi-Fi tracking to deliver targeted promotions and enhance customer experiences in retail stores, airports, and event venues. Conversely, outdoor geo-marketing targets consumers in public spaces, leveraging GPS and mobile data to deliver location-based advertising and promotions through digital billboards, apps, and social media, effectively driving foot traffic and brand awareness in real-time.</p></p>

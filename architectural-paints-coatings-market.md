<p><p>The architectural paints and coatings market is primarily categorized into water-borne, solvent-borne, and other types. Water-borne coatings are popular due to their low levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean. Solvent-borne coatings offer robust durability and are often used in high-performance applications but may release more VOCs. Other coatings may include specialty products like UV-cured or powder coatings, catering to specific requirements in commercial and residential applications.</p></p>

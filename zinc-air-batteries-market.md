<p><p>Zinc-air batteries are categorized into three market types. Secondary (rechargeable) zinc-air batteries are designed for applications where the ability to recharge is vital, typically featuring enhanced cycle life and performance. The mechanical recharge market involves systems that can restore energy through mechanical means, promoting sustainability and reducing waste in battery usage.</p></p>

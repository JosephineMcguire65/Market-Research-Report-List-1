<p><strong>The Automobile Weather Strip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EPDM Strip</li><li>TPE/TPO/TPV Strip</li><li>Other Strip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile weather strip market consists of three main types: EPDM strips, TPE/TPO/TPV strips, and other strips. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) strips are known for their excellent weather resistance and durability, making them ideal for sealing automotive components. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer), TPO (Thermoplastic Olefin), and TPV (Thermoplastic Vulcanizate) strips offer flexibility and recyclability, catering to diverse sealing needs. Other strips include various materials used for specialized applications, contributing to overall vehicle insulation and protection against environmental elements.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Cold Drawn</li><li>Hot Rolled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Precision Stainless Steel Strip market is divided into two primary types: cold drawn and hot rolled. Cold drawn stainless steel strips are produced by pulling metal through a die at room temperature, resulting in a tighter tolerance and smoother surface finish, ideal for precision applications. In contrast, hot rolled strips are made by rolling heated metal, resulting in larger tolerances and less surface quality, primarily used in applications where precise dimensions are less critical. Both types serve various industrial needs.</p></p>

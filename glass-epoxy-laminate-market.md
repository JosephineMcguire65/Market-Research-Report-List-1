<p><p>The Glass Epoxy Laminate market comprises several types, including G10, G11, FR4, FR5, and FR6, each with unique properties. G10 is a high-strength, moisture-resistant laminate, ideal for electrical applications. G11 offers superior thermal resistance and is used in high-temperature environments. FR4 is widely known for its excellent electrical insulation and is commonly used in circuit boards. FR5 provides enhanced thermal and electrical properties, while FR6, designed for high-frequency applications, offers low dielectric constant and loss. Each variant serves specific industrial needs, from electronics to aerospace.</p></p>

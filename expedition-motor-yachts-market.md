<p><strong>The Expedition Motor Yachts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Displacement Hull</li><li>Semi-Displacement Hull</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The expedition motor yachts market is primarily categorized into two hull types: displacement hulls and semi-displacement hulls. Displacement hulls are designed for efficient cruising at slower speeds, utilizing their hull shape to move through water, making them ideal for long-distance journeys and rough sea conditions. In contrast, semi-displacement hulls blend features of both displacement and planing designs, enabling them to operate effectively at both moderate and higher speeds, appealing to those seeking versatility for diverse cruising experiences.</p></p>

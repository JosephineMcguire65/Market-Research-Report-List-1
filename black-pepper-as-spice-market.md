<p><p>The black pepper spice market consists primarily of two main types: whole black pepper and black pepper powder. Whole black pepper refers to the dried, unground peppercorns, prized for their robust flavor and longer shelf life, making them ideal for seasoning dishes or as decorative elements. In contrast, black pepper powder, which is finely ground, offers convenience for quick culinary use and is often utilized in pre-packaged foods or as a staple seasoning, catering to the demand for ready-to-use spices.</p></p>

<p><p>The probiotic ingredients for animal use market can be segmented into three main types: bacteria, yeast, and spore formers. Bacteria, including species like Lactobacillus and Bifidobacterium, promote gut health and improve digestion. Yeast, often Saccharomyces, enhances nutrient absorption and supports immune function. Spore formers, such as Bacillus species, are heat-resistant and remain viable in challenging environments, providing sustained benefits. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing animal health, performance, and overall well-being in livestock and pets.</p></p>

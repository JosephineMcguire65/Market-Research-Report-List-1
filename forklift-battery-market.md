<p><p>The forklift battery market predominantly features two types: lead-acid batteries and lithium-ion (Li-ion) batteries. Lead-acid batteries are traditional, cost-effective, and widely used, known for their reliability and established infrastructure. However, they have longer charging times and shorter lifespans. In contrast, Li-ion batteries offer advantages such as faster charging, longer runtime, and reduced weight, making them increasingly popular in modern warehouse operations. Their higher initial cost is often offset by lower maintenance and operational expenses over time.</p></p>

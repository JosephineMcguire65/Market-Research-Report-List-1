<p><ul><li>Heat Sealable Solid White Film</li><li>Non-heat Sealable Solid White Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Solid White Film market is segmented into two main types: heat sealable and non-heat sealable films. Heat sealable solid white films are designed to bond securely when heat is applied, making them suitable for packaging applications requiring airtight seals. Non-heat sealable solid white films, on the other hand, do not require heat activation for sealing and are often used in applications where bonding is achieved through adhesives or mechanical means. Both types cater to diverse packaging needs across various industries.</p></p>

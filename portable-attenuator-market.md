<p><p>The portable attenuator market comprises three main types: variable, fixed, and programmable attenuators. Variable attenuators allow users to adjust signal strength dynamically, making them versatile for various applications. Fixed attenuators provide a constant level of attenuation, ideal for specific tasks where adjustments aren’t needed. Programmable attenuators offer advanced control, enabling users to set precise attenuation levels through digital interfaces, ideal for automated systems. Each type serves unique needs in communications, testing, and measurement environments.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Motorsport Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motorsport tire market is primarily divided into radial and bias tire types. Radial tires feature steel belts running perpendicular to the tread, providing superior grip, stability, and heat dissipation, making them ideal for high-performance racing. Bias tires, on the other hand, consist of layers of fabric crisscrossing under the tread, resulting in a stiffer sidewall and better flexibility, often preferred for older racing formats or specific track conditions. Each type caters to distinct racing needs and preferences.</p></p>

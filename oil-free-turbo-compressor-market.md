<p><p>The oil-free turbo-compressor market is categorized into single stroke and two stroke turbo compressors, each designed for specific applications. Single stroke turbo compressors utilize a single cycle to compress gas, providing efficiency and reduced wear, making them ideal for continuous operations. In contrast, two stroke turbo compressors employ a double cycle, allowing for higher power output and enhanced performance in demanding conditions. Both types are essential in industries where oil contamination must be avoided, such as pharmaceuticals and food processing.</p></p>

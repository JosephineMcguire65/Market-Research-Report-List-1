<p><p>The Vehicle Electric Fuel Pump market includes various types such as Roller Pump, Gear Pump, Vane Pump, and Others. Roller Pumps use rotating rollers to move fuel, offering durability and efficiency. Gear Pumps utilize interlocking gears for precise fuel delivery, ideal for high-pressure systems. Vane Pumps employ sliding vanes to create suction, providing smooth and consistent fuel flow. Other types include advanced designs catering to specific vehicle needs. Each type serves distinct applications, balancing performance, reliability, and fuel efficiency in modern automotive systems.</p></p>

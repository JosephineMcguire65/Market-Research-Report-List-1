<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laboratory Biosensors Market is typically categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I biosensors primarily focus on detecting specific biological parameters, often employing optical or electrochemical techniques for accurate measurements. In contrast, Type II biosensors are designed for broader application, capable of monitoring various biochemical substances simultaneously, utilizing advanced technologies like microfluidics and nanotechnology. Both types play crucial roles in research, clinical diagnostics, and environmental monitoring, aiding in rapid and precise analysis of biological samples.</p></p>

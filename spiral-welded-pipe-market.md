<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Spiral Welded Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ERW Pipes</li><li>LSAW Pipes</li><li>SSAW Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spiral welded pipe market comprises three main types: ERW (Electric Resistance Welded) pipes, LSAW (Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welded) pipes, and SSAW (Spiral Submerged Arc Welded) pipes. ERW pipes are created by rolling and welding flat steel sheets, suitable for low-pressure applications. LSAW pipes, which are made from heavy plates, are ideal for high-pressure projects. SSAW pipes are formed by spirally welding steel strips, offering flexibility for various diameters and lengths, catering to diverse infrastructure and energy needs.</p></p>

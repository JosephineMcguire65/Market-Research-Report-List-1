<p><p>The NB-IoT chipset market comprises three main types: Standalone, In-Band, and Guard Band. Standalone operates independently within its own spectrum, offering enhanced performance and coverage. In-Band utilizes existing LTE channels, enabling seamless integration into current networks while minimizing disruption. Guard Band, on the other hand, functions within the unused frequencies between LTE channels, optimizing spectrum usage. Each type caters to different deployment scenarios, balancing factors like efficiency, network infrastructure, and deployment flexibility in Internet of Things applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The stimulation materials market is primarily divided into two key segments: proppants and chemicals. Proppants are solid materials, such as sand or resin-coated particles, used to keep fractures open in oil and gas wells, enhancing hydrocarbon flow. Chemicals, on the other hand, include various additives that improve the efficiency of hydraulic fracturing, such as friction reducers, biocides, and gelling agents. Together, these materials play a crucial role in optimizing extraction processes and maximizing production in the energy sector.</p></p>

<p><p>The medical X-ray devices market is primarily divided into two types: analog X-ray and digital X-ray systems. Analog X-ray devices use traditional film to capture images, offering a straightforward imaging process but requiring physical film development. In contrast, digital X-ray systems utilize electronic sensors to capture and instantly display images on screens, allowing for enhanced image quality, reduced radiation exposure, and easier storage and sharing. Digital systems are increasingly favored for their efficiency and advanced imaging capabilities.</p></p>

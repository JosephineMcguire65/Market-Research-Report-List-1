<p><p>Map Positioning Units are categorized into two primary market types based on their accuracy: centimeter-level positioning units and meter-level positioning units. Centimeter-level positioning units offer high precision for applications like autonomous vehicles and surveying, enabling accurate location tracking within centimeters. In contrast, meter-level positioning units provide less precise positioning, suitable for general navigation and outdoor activities. The choice between these units depends on the specific requirements of applications, with centimeter-level units targeting high accuracy needs and meter-level units serving broader use cases.</p></p>

<p><p>The laser systems market is primarily categorized into two types: continuous laser systems and pulsed laser systems. Continuous lasers emit a constant beam of light, making them ideal for applications requiring consistent and high-intensity output, such as cutting and welding. In contrast, pulsed laser systems generate light in short bursts, which allows for precision in tasks like medical procedures and material processing. Each type serves distinct industries, contributing to advancements in manufacturing, healthcare, telecommunications, and more.</p></p>

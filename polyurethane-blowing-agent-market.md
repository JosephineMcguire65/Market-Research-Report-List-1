<p><p>The polyurethane blowing agent market includes two primary types: tube and gun-style systems. Tube systems utilize pre-measured cartridges that deliver blowing agents directly into the mixing chamber, offering convenience and precision for small-scale applications. In contrast, gun-style systems employ a spray mechanism to introduce blowing agents, providing flexibility for larger projects and more extensive foam applications. Both types cater to various industries, including construction and automotive, while ensuring efficient foam production and insulation properties.</p></p>

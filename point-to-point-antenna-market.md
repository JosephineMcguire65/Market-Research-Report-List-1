<p><p>The Point-to-Point Antenna market comprises three main types. Parabolic antennas, characterized by their dish-shaped design, excel in long-range communication with high gain. Flat panel antennas offer a compact, low-profile alternative, providing reliable performance for both urban and rural applications. Yagi antennas, with their directional capabilities and simple construction, are commonly used for shorter-range communications. Each type serves specific needs within the market, catering to various industries such as telecommunications, broadcasting, and satellite communications.</p></p>

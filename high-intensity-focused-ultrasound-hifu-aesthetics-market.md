<p><p>The High Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) aesthetics market can be categorized into two main types: ultrasound-guided and MR-guided HIFU. Ultrasound-guided HIFU utilizes real-time ultrasound imaging to precisely target tissue, ensuring accurate delivery of energy to the desired depth, ideal for skin tightening and lifting procedures. In contrast, MR-guided HIFU employs magnetic resonance imaging to visualize and plan treatment areas, providing enhanced precision for deeper tissue applications. Both methods aim to achieve non-invasive aesthetic improvements with minimal downtime.</p></p>

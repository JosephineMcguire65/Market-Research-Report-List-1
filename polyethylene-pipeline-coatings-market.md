<p><ul><li>Internal Coating</li><li>External Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polyethylene Pipeline Coatings market is divided into two main types: internal coatings and external coatings. Internal coatings are applied to the inner surfaces of pipelines to reduce friction, minimize corrosion, and enhance the flow of liquids. External coatings, on the other hand, protect the outer surfaces from environmental factors and physical damage, thereby extending the pipeline's lifespan. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the durability and efficiency of pipeline systems in various industries.</p></p>

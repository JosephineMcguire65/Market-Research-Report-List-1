<p><p>The centrifugal submersible pumps market comprises two main types: single-stage pumps and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed to handle low head applications, providing efficient fluid transfer at moderate flow rates, making them ideal for drainage and sewage operations. Multi-stage pumps, on the other hand, consist of multiple impellers, enabling them to generate higher pressure and elevate fluids to greater heights, suitable for applications requiring increased pressure, such as water supply and irrigation, enhancing their versatility in various industries.</p></p>

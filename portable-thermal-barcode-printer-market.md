<p><strong>The Portable Thermal Barcode Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Thermal</li><li>Thermal Transfer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable thermal barcode printer market is primarily divided into two types: direct thermal and thermal transfer. Direct thermal printers use heat-sensitive paper, producing images through heat application, making them ideal for short-term labeling due to their susceptibility to fading. Conversely, thermal transfer printers utilize a ribbon to transfer ink onto the substrate, resulting in more durable prints suitable for long-lasting applications. Each type serves different needs, with direct thermal being cost-effective and thermal transfer offering longevity and resilience.</p></p>

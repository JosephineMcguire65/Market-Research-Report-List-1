<p><p>The fluoropolymer market encompasses several key types, each with unique properties and applications. PVDF (polyvinylidene fluoride) offers superior thermal stability and is commonly used in the chemical processing and electrical industries. Other fluoropolymers include various specialized grades designed for niche applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The automotive all-wheel drive (AWD) market features two primary types: on-demand all-wheel drive and full-time all-wheel drive. On-demand AWD activates automatically when additional traction is needed, optimizing fuel efficiency during normal driving conditions. Full-time AWD, on the other hand, continuously powers all four wheels, providing enhanced stability and traction in various driving conditions. Both systems improve vehicle handling and safety, catering to different consumer preferences for performance, versatility, and efficiency in various terrains.</p></p>

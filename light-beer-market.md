<p><strong>The Light Beer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Limit Fermentation</li><li>Dealcoholization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light beer market can be categorized into two primary types based on production techniques: limit fermentation and dealcoholization methods. Limit fermentation involves reducing the yeast activity during the brewing process, resulting in lower alcohol content while maintaining flavor. In contrast, dealcoholization methods focus on removing alcohol from fully fermented beer, preserving the original taste while achieving a lighter beverage. Both methods cater to health-conscious consumers seeking lower-calorie options without sacrificing the beer-drinking experience.</p></p>

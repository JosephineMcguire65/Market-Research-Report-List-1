<p><p>The automotive transmission market encompasses various types to enhance vehicle performance and efficiency. Manual Transmission (MT) offers direct driver control, while Automatic Transmission (AT) simplifies gear shifts for ease of use. Automated Manual Transmission (AMT) combines features of both MT and AT, allowing semi-automatic control. Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) provides seamless acceleration by varying gear ratios, and Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT) offers rapid gear changes for improved responsiveness. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and driving conditions.</p></p>

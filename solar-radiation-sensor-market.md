<p><ul><li>Silicon Cell Type</li><li>Thermopile Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar radiation sensor market comprises two main types: silicon cell sensors and thermopile sensors. Silicon cell sensors utilize photovoltaic technology to convert solar radiation into an electrical current, making them effective for measuring solar energy in various applications. Thermopile sensors, on the other hand, employ a series of thermocouples to detect temperature changes caused by solar irradiation, offering high sensitivity and accuracy. Both types are essential for optimizing solar energy systems and enhancing climate monitoring capabilities.</p></p>

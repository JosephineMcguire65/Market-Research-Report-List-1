<p><p>The Type A molecular sieve market consists of three primary categories: 3A, 4A, and 5A molecular sieves, each differing in their pore sizes and adsorption properties. 3A molecular sieves have a pore size of 3 angstroms, making them ideal for selectively adsorbing small molecules like water. 4A sieves, with pore sizes of 4 angstroms, can adsorb a broader range of molecules, including water and some gases. 5A sieves feature a 5 angstrom pore size, enabling them to capture larger molecules such as hydrocarbons.</p></p>

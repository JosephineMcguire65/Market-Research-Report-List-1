<p><p>Aluminum oxide ceramic substrates are categorized by their alumina content: 95%, 97%, and 99% alumina. The 95% alumina type is cost-effective and offers good electrical insulation and thermal conductivity, suitable for standard applications. The 97% alumina variant provides enhanced mechanical strength and thermal stability, making it ideal for mid-range applications. The 99% alumina substrate, with its superior purity, delivers exceptional thermal and electrical properties, often used in high-performance and demanding environments, such as aerospace and electronics.</p></p>

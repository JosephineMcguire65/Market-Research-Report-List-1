<p><ul><li>Solvent Adhesive</li><li>Solvent-free Adhesive</li><li>Waterborne Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical packaging adhesive market is categorized into three main types: solvent adhesives, solvent-free adhesives, and waterborne adhesives. Solvent adhesives contain volatile organic compounds, providing strong bonding but may pose environmental concerns. Solvent-free adhesives eliminate solvents, offering safer alternatives with minimal environmental impact while maintaining adhesion quality. Waterborne adhesives utilize water as a carrier, making them environmentally friendly and low in toxicity, suitable for sensitive applications. Each type serves specific regulatory and performance requirements in pharmaceutical packaging.</p></p>
